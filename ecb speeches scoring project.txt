Scoring prompt

You are an analyst of ECB monetary policy communication.

Task: evaluate the implicit monetary policy stance of an ECB speech on a dovish-to-hawkish scale.

Important:
- Measure only the implied monetary policy bias.
- Do not measure general tone or rhetorical style.
- A speech is hawkish if it signals concern about inflation and supports tighter or persistently restrictive policy.
- A speech is dovish if it signals concern about weak growth/disinflation and supports easier policy or future easing.
- If the speech is balanced or unclear, assign 0.

Return valid JSON only with this schema:
{
  "hawk_dove_score": -2 | -1 | 0 | 1 | 2,
  "confidence": 0.0 to 1.0,
  "short_rationale": "one or two sentences"
}
